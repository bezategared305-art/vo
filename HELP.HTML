Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about online voting

How do I know my vote is secure?

Our system uses military-grade end-to-end encryption, anonymous voting IDs, and blockchain verification. Each vote is encrypted before leaving your device and can only be decrypted by the official counting servers. You receive a unique verification code to confirm your vote was counted.

What if I make a mistake on my ballot?

You can review and change your selections as many times as needed before final submission. Once you click "Submit," your vote is final and cannot be changed. Take your time reviewing each selection on the confirmation page.

Can I vote from my mobile phone?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices - smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. We recommend using a secure, private internet connection regardless of device.

What if I lose internet connection while voting?

Your progress is automatically saved every 30 seconds. If you lose connection, simply log back in and you'll be returned to where you left off. You have 30 minutes of inactivity before you'll need to start over.

How do I verify my vote was counted?

After voting, you'll receive a unique, anonymous verification code. You can enter this code on our "Track My Vote" page to confirm your ballot was received and counted. This verification is available 24 hours after voting closes.

Common Issues & Solutions

"Invalid Credentials" Error

Double-check your username and password. Use the "Forgot Password" link if needed. Ensure Caps Lock is off.

Session Timeout

For security, sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity. Simply log back in to continue voting.

Browser Compatibility

Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. Clear cache/cookies if experiencing issues.

Mobile Display Issues

Rotate your device to landscape mode for better viewing. Ensure you have the latest OS updates.
