Legal & Privacy Policy

Governance framework for the E-Voting System and Public Results Platform

1. Purpose of This Platform

This e-voting platform is designed to provide a secure, transparent, and reliable electronic voting and public results system. The public results section is strictly read-only and is intended for informational purposes only.

2. Data Collection & Privacy

The system respects user privacy and data protection principles. The public results page does not collect, store, or process any personal data from visitors. For voting participants, only the minimum necessary information required for voter authentication and election integrity is processed.

3. Vote Confidentiality

Individual votes are anonymous and confidential. No vote can be traced back to any individual voter. Published results contain only aggregated totals.

4. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect election data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or loss.

5. Accuracy of Results

All published results represent officially recorded and verified vote counts at the time of publication. While every effort is made to ensure accuracy, the administrators reserve the right to correct errors if identified.

6. Legal Disclaimer

This platform is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind. The system administrators shall not be held liable for any damages arising from the use or inability to use this platform.

7. Unauthorized Use

Any attempt to manipulate, disrupt, or gain unauthorized access to the system is strictly prohibited and may result in legal action.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Legal & Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Information

For questions regarding this policy or the operation of the system, please contact the system administrator or the responsible electoral authority.
