Accessibility Features

  • WCAG 2.1 AA Compliant - Meets international web accessibility standards
  • Screen Reader Ready - Compatible with JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, and TalkBack
  • Keyboard Navigation - Complete website functionality without a mouse
  • High Contrast Mode - Enhanced visibility for low vision users
  • Text-to-Speech - Built-in read-aloud functionality
  • Text Resizing - Adjust font size up to 200% without breaking layout
  • Captioning & Transcripts - All multimedia content includes captions
  • Dyslexia-Friendly Font - Option to use dyslexia-friendly typography
  • Simplified Language - Clear, straightforward instructions
  • Focus Indicators - Clear visual focus for keyboard navigation

Special Assistance Options

In-Person Assistance

Visit any polling center for trained accessibility specialists who can assist with voting.

Telephone Voting

Call 1-800-VOTE-NOW to cast your ballot by phone with operator assistance.

Braille Ballots

Request a Braille ballot by contacting your local election office at least 2 weeks in advance.

ASL Interpreters

American Sign Language interpreters available at all major polling locations.
